About Me

I am a JavaScript developer who spends most of my time writing code, breaking it, fixing it, and understanding why it behaved the way it did.

I work primarily with JavaScript, focusing on building applications while improving my understanding of how things work under the hood rather than relying on surface-level solutions.

What keeps me engaged in development is problem-solving. I enjoy taking unclear problems, breaking them into smaller parts, and turning them into clear, working solutions.

I value strong fundamentals and clean, readable code. I prefer learning concepts properly instead of making things work by accident.

Consistency drives my progress. Writing code every day, learning from mistakes, and refining my approach over time helps me grow as a developer.

This website exists as a record of my journey — the things I am building, the ideas I am exploring, and the skills I am developing.

It is not a finished product. Neither am I.
